Workshop Details

What You'll Learn in 21 Days

Our 21-Day Online Yoga Workshop is designed to help you build healthy habits through simple, practical, and effective yoga techniques.

During the workshop, you'll practice:

  • Yoga fundamentals and proper alignment
  • Gentle warm-up techniques
  • Joint mobility exercises
  • Surya Namaskar with different variations
  • Simple and effective yoga asanas
  • Core strengthening practices
  • Yoga for better digestion and healthy elimination
  • Tapping techniques for relaxation and body awareness
  • Breathing exercises (Pranayama)
  • Guided relaxation and meditation
  • Daily yoga habits for a healthy lifestyle
  • Healthy hydration and water-drinking habits
  • Basic Satvic food and lifestyle guidance
  • Tips to improve flexibility, posture, and balance
  • Simple techniques to reduce stress and increase energy
  • Safe home practice with step-by-step guidance

What's Included

  • Live online yoga sessions
  • Session recordings (available if you miss a class)
  • Presentation materials and practice guidance
  • Daily motivation and support throughout the program

What You'll Need

  • Yoga mat
  • Two yoga blocks (recommended)
  • Yoga strap or belt
  • Yoga bolster or firm pillow (optional)
  • Yoga towel or blanket
  • Water bottle
  • Comfortable clothing suitable for movement
  • A quiet, well-ventilated practice space
  • A stable internet connection
  • Keep your camera (video) on during live sessions for posture guidance and personalized feedback

Benefits You May Experience

  • Improved flexibility
  • Better posture
  • Increased strength and balance
  • Better digestion
  • More energy throughout the day
  • Reduced stress
  • Improved focus and mental clarity
  • Healthier daily habits
  • Greater confidence in your yoga practice
