Curriculum Overview

Progressive 21-Day Learning Path

A systematic curriculum that starts with basic joint conditioning and advances into holistic physical postures, breath work, and relaxation.

Days 1 - 5

Joint Mobility & Conditioning

We begin by preparing the skeletal and muscular frameworks. You will master basic warm-up sequences, joint mobility exercises (Sukshma Vyayama), and dynamic postures to build systemic circulation.

  • Neck, shoulder, and wrist opening routines
  • Ankle and knee stabilization sequences
  • Introductory breathing coordination patterns
Days 6 - 12

Spinal Realignment & Yoga Postures (Asanas)

Transition into foundational standing, seated, and prone yoga postures. Focus on spine extension, stability, and corrective alignment to relieve back compression and daily posture imbalances.

  • Standing postures for building lower body stability
  • Seated twists for visceral detoxification
  • Prone chest-openers to strengthen dorsal muscles
Days 13 - 17

Breathing Mastery (Pranayama) & Internal Cleanse

Deepen your practice by focusing on the respiratory system. Learn standard pranayama (breath control) methods to clean neural passages, balance the cerebral hemispheres, and control stress responses.

  • Abdominal breathing techniques (Diaphragmatic breath)
  • Alternate Nostril Breathing (Nadi Shodhana)
  • Cooling and energizing pranayamas for mental clarity
Days 18 - 21

Mindfulness, Relaxation & Integration

Consolidate your learning. Integrate dynamic flows, deep relaxation practices (Yoga Nidra), and silent awareness techniques to develop a sustainable self-guided yoga routine.

  • Systematic muscle-release relaxation (Shavasana)
  • Guided visualization and gratitude meditations
  • Creating your customized home yoga schedule
